How to unsubscribe?
1. Open an email message sent by CodingRoutine. You will find it here.
2. At the bottom of the email content search for unsubscribe button.
3. After clicking it, you will no longer receive any emails from CodingRoutine. If you change your mind, you can always sign up again!
